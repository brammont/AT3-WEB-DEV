Cossack

Cossack, Western Australia (Bajinhurrba)

Cossack is a historic town located in Western Australia, known for its rich heritage and stunning coastal views. Explore the town's heritage buildings, museums, and scenic walking trails. Visit the Cossack Art Awards and immerse yourself in the local arts scene.

Culinary Masterclass with Head Chef Sophia

Guests can participate in an interactive cooking class led by Head Chef Sophia Reynolds. Learn to prepare signature dishes using locally sourced ingredients and discover the secrets behind Blue Springs Hotel's exquisite cuisine.

Wellness Retreat at the Spa

Indulge in a rejuvenating wellness retreat at the hotel's spa, managed by Natalie Evans. Enjoy a range of holistic treatments, from massages to facials, designed to promote relaxation and rejuvenation in a tranquil setting.

Guided Aboriginal Cultural Experience

Immerse yourself in the rich indigenous heritage of the Ngarluma people with a guided cultural experience. Led by local elders, this activity offers insights into traditional practices, storytelling, and art, providing a deeper understanding of the region's history and culture.

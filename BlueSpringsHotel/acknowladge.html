Acknowledgement of Country

In the Acknowledgment of Country, we respectfully recognize and pay tribute to the Ngarluma people, the traditional custodians of this land.

Go to Youtube Channel.
Michael Thompson - Front Office Manager

The Ngarluma Foundation is an Indigenous Australian organisation aiming to empower the Ngarluma people through cultural preservation, education, and land management initiatives.

They work to protect the Ngarluma heritage and country, while fostering a sustainable future for the Ngarluma community. Their efforts include cultural education programs, land and sea management projects, and advocating for Ngarluma rights and interests.
